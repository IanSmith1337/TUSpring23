# Practice

## For (0, 0)

- Scale:

$$ \begin{pmatrix}   2(0) & 0 & 0  \\ 0 & 2(0) & 0 \\   0 & 0 & 1 \end{pmatrix} $$

- Add Translate:

$$ \begin{pmatrix}   2(0) & 0 & -1  \\ 0 & 2(0) & 0 \\   0 & 0 & 1 \end{pmatrix} $$

- Result:

$$ \begin{pmatrix}   0 & 0 & -1  \\ 0 & 0 & 0 \\   0 & 0 & 1 \end{pmatrix} $$  

## For (1, 1):

- Result:
